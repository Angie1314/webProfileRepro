The Horse Head Nebula Fun Fact

The Horsehead Nebula will eventually disperse due to ultra violet light from nearby stars evaporating its gas cloud. In 2001 the Horshead Nebula was voted the favorite night sky object by amateur astronomers.

Black Tip Shark Fun Fact

These sharks are common on tropical Indo-Pacific coral reefs in shallow, coastal waters. At the surface, it’s easy to spot their exposed first dorsal fins.

Mt. Kelimutu Fun Fact

The three lakes are named Tiwu Ata Mbupu, Tiwu Nuwa Muri Koo Fai, and Tiwu Ata Polo, which translate to Lake of Old People, Lake of Young Men and Maidens, and Bewitched or Enchanted Lake, respectively.

Blue Stone Beach Flores

This Blue Stone Beach in Ende is composed almost entirely of unusual turquoise, blue, and reddish stones. Locally known as Penggajawa Beach, you’ll see the Blue Stone Beach at the scenic 25-kilometre drive northwest from Ende on the road to Bajawa that takes about 30 or 40 minutes.

UFO Sighting Fun Facts

A UFO sighting in the Southern California night sky turned out to be an Elon Musk SpaceX rocket on a satellite mission, but only after freaking out some on the ground, including celebrities.

Chihuahua Fun Facts

Chihuahuas are believed to be descendants of an ancient breed, the techichi. Toltecs in Mexico kept these pups as lap dogs as early as the 9th century CE.
